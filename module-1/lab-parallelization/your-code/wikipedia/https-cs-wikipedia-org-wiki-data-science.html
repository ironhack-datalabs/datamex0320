Data science neboli datov\xc3\xa1 v\xc4\x9bda je interdisciplin\xc3\xa1rn\xc3\xad obor, kter\xc3\xbd vyu\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadv\xc3\xa1 v\xc4\x9bdeck\xc3\xa9 metody, procesy, algoritmy a syst\xc3\xa9my pro z\xc3\xadsk\xc3\xa1v\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad znalost\xc3\xad a poznatk\xc5\xaf z dat v r\xc5\xafzn\xc3\xbdch podob\xc3\xa1ch, jak strukturovan\xc3\xbdch, tak nestrukturovan\xc3\xbdch[1][2] podobn\xc4\x9b jako data mining.\n

Data science "sjednocuje statistiku, anal\xc3\xbdzu dat, strojov\xc3\xa9 u\xc4\x8den\xc3\xad a souvisej\xc3\xadc\xc3\xad metody" s c\xc3\xadlem "pochopit a analyzovat skute\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xa9 jevy" na z\xc3\xa1klad\xc4\x9b dat.[3] Vyu\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadv\xc3\xa1 techniky a teorie \xc4\x8derpan\xc3\xa9 z mnoha oblast\xc3\xad matematiky, statistiky, informatiky a matematick\xc3\xa9 informatiky.\n

Nositel Turingovy ceny Jim Gray si data science p\xc5\x99edstavoval jako \xe2\x80\x9e\xc4\x8dtvrt\xc3\xa9 paradigma\xe2\x80\x9c v\xc4\x9bdy (empirick\xc3\xa9, teoretick\xc3\xa9, v\xc3\xbdpo\xc4\x8detn\xc3\xad a nyn\xc3\xad zalo\xc5\xbeen\xc3\xa9 na datech) a tvrdil, \xc5\xbee \xe2\x80\x9ev\xc5\xa1echno ve v\xc4\x9bd\xc4\x9b se m\xc4\x9bn\xc3\xad v d\xc5\xafsledku vlivu informa\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xadch technologi\xc3\xad\xe2\x80\x9c a z\xc3\xa1plavy dat.[4][5]\n

V roce 2012, kdy Harvard Business Review nazval data science "nejv\xc3\xadce sexy zam\xc4\x9bstn\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xadm 21. stolet\xc3\xad"[6] se tento term\xc3\xadn stal m\xc3\xb3dn\xc3\xadm slovem. To je nyn\xc3\xad \xc4\x8dasto pou\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadv\xc3\xa1no zam\xc4\x9bniteln\xc4\x9b se star\xc5\xa1\xc3\xadmi pojmy jako business analytics,[7] business intelligence, prediktivn\xc3\xad modelov\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad, data mining a statistika. Dokonce i my\xc5\xa1lenka, \xc5\xbee datov\xc3\xa1 v\xc4\x9bda je sexy, parafr\xc3\xa1zuje Hanse Roslinga, kter\xc3\xbd v dokumentu BBC 2011 prohl\xc3\xa1sil: "Statistika je nyn\xc3\xad nejv\xc3\xadce sexy t\xc3\xa9ma, kter\xc3\xa9 tu m\xc3\xa1me."[8] Nate Silver ozna\xc4\x8dil datovou v\xc4\x9bdu za sexy term\xc3\xadn pro statistiku.[9] V mnoha p\xc5\x99\xc3\xadpadech jsou nyn\xc3\xad d\xc5\x99\xc3\xadv\xc4\x9bj\xc5\xa1\xc3\xad p\xc5\x99\xc3\xadstupy a \xc5\x99e\xc5\xa1en\xc3\xad jednodu\xc5\xa1e p\xc5\x99ejmenov\xc3\xa1v\xc3\xa1ny jako data science, aby byly atraktivn\xc4\x9bj\xc5\xa1\xc3\xad, co\xc5\xbe m\xc5\xaf\xc5\xbee zp\xc5\xafsobit, \xc5\xbee term\xc3\xadn se "z\xc5\x99ed\xc3\xad [...] za hranici u\xc5\xbeite\xc4\x8dnosti".[10] I kdy\xc5\xbe mnoh\xc3\xa9 univerzitn\xc3\xad programy nyn\xc3\xad nab\xc3\xadzej\xc3\xad titul v oboru datov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdy, neexistuje konsensus o jej\xc3\xad definici nebo o obsahu u\xc4\x8debn\xc3\xadch osnov.[7] K diskreditaci data science p\xc5\x99isp\xc3\xadv\xc3\xa1 mnoho projekt\xc5\xaf v oblasti datov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdy a velk\xc3\xbdch dat, kter\xc3\xa9 nedok\xc3\xa1zaly poskytnout u\xc5\xbeite\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xa9 v\xc3\xbdsledky, \xc4\x8dasto v d\xc5\xafsledku \xc5\xa1patn\xc3\xa9ho \xc5\x99\xc3\xadzen\xc3\xad a vyu\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadv\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad zdroj\xc5\xaf.[11][12][13][14]\n

Term\xc3\xadn datov\xc3\xa1 v\xc4\x9bda se objevoval v r\xc5\xafzn\xc3\xbdch souvislostech u\xc5\xbe des\xc3\xadtky let, ale a\xc5\xbe doned\xc3\xa1vna se neust\xc3\xa1lil. D\xc5\x99\xc3\xadve byl pou\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadv\xc3\xa1n jako synonymum informatiky Peterem Naurem v roce 1960. Naur pozd\xc4\x9bji p\xc5\x99edstavil term\xc3\xadn "datalogy".[15] V roce 1974 vydal Naur knihu Concise Survey of Computer Methods (Stru\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xbd p\xc5\x99ehled po\xc4\x8d\xc3\xadta\xc4\x8dov\xc3\xbdch metod), kde voln\xc4\x9b vyu\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadval term\xc3\xadn data science v p\xc5\x99ehledu sou\xc4\x8dasn\xc3\xbdch metod zpracov\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad dat pou\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadvan\xc3\xbdch v \xc5\xa1irok\xc3\xa9 \xc5\xa1k\xc3\xa1le aplikac\xc3\xad.\n

V roce 1996 se \xc4\x8dlenov\xc3\xa9 Mezin\xc3\xa1rodn\xc3\xad federace klasifika\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xadch spole\xc4\x8dnost\xc3\xad (Federation of Classification Societies, IFCS) se\xc5\xa1li v K\xc3\xb3be na konferenci konan\xc3\xa9 ka\xc5\xbed\xc3\xa9 dva roky. Zde se poprv\xc3\xa9 term\xc3\xadn data science objevil v n\xc3\xa1zvu konference (Data Science, classification, and related methods - V\xc4\x9bda o datech, klasifikace a souvisej\xc3\xadc\xc3\xad metody).[16][17] pot\xc3\xa9, co byl zaveden v neform\xc3\xa1ln\xc3\xad diskusi Chikiem Hayashim.[3]\n

V listopadu 1997 p\xc5\x99ednesl C. F. Jeff Wu inaugura\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xad p\xc5\x99edn\xc3\xa1\xc5\xa1ku nazvanou "Statistics = Data Science?"[18] u p\xc5\x99\xc3\xadle\xc5\xbeitosti jmenov\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad profesorem Michigansk\xc3\xa9 univerzity.[19] V p\xc5\x99edn\xc3\xa1\xc5\xa1ce charakterizoval statistickou pr\xc3\xa1ci jako trojici sb\xc4\x9bru dat, modelov\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad \xc4\x8di anal\xc3\xbdzy dat a rozhodov\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad. Na z\xc3\xa1v\xc4\x9br uvedl modern\xc3\xad pou\xc5\xbeit\xc3\xad term\xc3\xadnu data science mimo kontext informatiky a navrhl p\xc5\x99ejmenov\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad statistiky na data science a statistik\xc5\xaf na data scientisty, datov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdce.[18] Pozd\xc4\x9bji p\xc5\x99edn\xc3\xa1\xc5\xa1ku "Statistics = Data Science"? zopakoval roku 1998 jako prvn\xc3\xad ze sv\xc3\xbdch Mahalanobisovsk\xc3\xbdch p\xc5\x99edn\xc3\xa1\xc5\xa1ek.[20]\n

V roce 2001 William S. Cleveland uvedl datovou v\xc4\x9bdu jako nez\xc3\xa1vislou discipl\xc3\xadnu, kter\xc3\xa1 roz\xc5\xa1i\xc5\x99uje oblast statistiky tak, aby zahrnovala "pokroky v oblasti v\xc3\xbdpo\xc4\x8detn\xc3\xad techniky s daty". My\xc5\xa1lenku uve\xc5\x99ejnil v \xc4\x8dl\xc3\xa1nku "Data Science: An Action Plan for Expanding the Technical Areas of the Field of Statistics" ("Data Science: ak\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xad pl\xc3\xa1n roz\xc5\xa1i\xc5\x99ov\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad technick\xc3\xbdch oblast\xc3\xad statistiky"), kter\xc3\xbd byl publikov\xc3\xa1n ve svazku 69, \xc4\x8d. 1, dubnov\xc3\xa9ho vyd\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad International Statistical Review / Revue Internationale de Statistique z dubna 2001.[21] Cleveland zde stanovil \xc5\xa1est oblast\xc3\xad, o kter\xc3\xbdch v\xc4\x9b\xc5\x99il, \xc5\xbee zahrnuj\xc3\xad data science: multidisciplin\xc3\xa1rn\xc3\xad b\xc3\xa1d\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad, modely a metody pro zpracov\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad dat, v\xc3\xbdpo\xc4\x8dty s daty, pedagogika, hodnocen\xc3\xad v\xc3\xbdzkumn\xc3\xbdch n\xc3\xa1stroj\xc5\xaf a teorie.\n

V dubnu 2002 zah\xc3\xa1jila Mezin\xc3\xa1rodn\xc3\xad rada pro v\xc4\x9bdu (International Council for Science, ICSU) a jej\xc3\xad V\xc3\xbdbor pro data pro v\xc4\x9bdu a technologii (Committee on Data for Science and Technology, CODATA)[22] vyd\xc3\xa1v\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad \xc4\x8dasopisu Data Science Journal[23] zam\xc4\x9b\xc5\x99en\xc3\xa9ho na ot\xc3\xa1zky, jako je popis datov\xc3\xbdch syst\xc3\xa9m\xc5\xaf, na internetu, aplikace a pr\xc3\xa1vn\xc3\xad ot\xc3\xa1zky.[24] Kr\xc3\xa1tce pot\xc3\xa9, v lednu 2003, Columbijsk\xc3\xa1 univerzita za\xc4\x8dala publikovat \xc4\x8dasopis Journal of Data Science[25] kter\xc3\xbd poskytl platformu v\xc5\xa1em datov\xc3\xbdm pracovn\xc3\xadk\xc5\xafm, aby prezentovali sv\xc3\xa9 n\xc3\xa1zory a vym\xc4\x9b\xc5\x88ovali si n\xc3\xa1pady. \xc4\x8casopis byl z velk\xc3\xa9 \xc4\x8d\xc3\xa1sti v\xc4\x9bnov\xc3\xa1n aplikaci statistick\xc3\xbdch metod a kvantitativn\xc3\xadho v\xc3\xbdzkumu. N\xc3\xa1rodn\xc3\xad v\xc4\x9bdeck\xc3\xa1 rada (National Science Board) vydala v roce 2005 text "Long-lived Digital Data Collections: Enabling Research and Education in the 21st Century" ("Dlouhodob\xc3\xbd sb\xc4\x9br digit\xc3\xa1ln\xc3\xadch dat: umo\xc5\xben\xc4\x9bn\xc3\xad v\xc3\xbdzkumu a vzd\xc4\x9bl\xc3\xa1v\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad v 21. stolet\xc3\xad"), definuj\xc3\xadc\xc3\xad datov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdce jako "informa\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xad a po\xc4\x8d\xc3\xadta\xc4\x8dov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdce, datab\xc3\xa1zov\xc3\xa9 a softwarov\xc3\xa9 tv\xc5\xafrce a program\xc3\xa1tory, experty jednotliv\xc3\xbdch discipl\xc3\xadn, kur\xc3\xa1tory a odborn\xc3\xad anot\xc3\xa1tory, knihovn\xc3\xadky, archiv\xc3\xa1\xc5\x99e a dal\xc5\xa1\xc3\xad, kte\xc5\x99\xc3\xad maj\xc3\xad z\xc3\xa1sadn\xc3\xad v\xc3\xbdznam pro \xc3\xbasp\xc4\x9b\xc5\xa1n\xc3\xa9 \xc5\x99\xc3\xadzen\xc3\xad sb\xc4\x9bru digit\xc3\xa1ln\xc3\xadch dat", jejich\xc5\xbe hlavn\xc3\xad \xc4\x8dinnost\xc3\xad je" prov\xc3\xa1d\xc4\x9bt tv\xc5\xafr\xc4\x8d\xc3\xad v\xc3\xbdzkum a anal\xc3\xbdzu".[26]\n

Okolo roku 2007 Jim Gray p\xc5\x99edstavil \xe2\x80\x9ev\xc4\x9bdu \xc5\x99\xc3\xadzenou daty\xe2\x80\x9c jako \xe2\x80\x9e\xc4\x8dtvrt\xc3\xa9 paradigma\xe2\x80\x9c v\xc4\x9bdy, kter\xc3\xa9 vyu\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadv\xc3\xa1 po\xc4\x8d\xc3\xadta\xc4\x8dovou anal\xc3\xbdzu velk\xc3\xbdch dat jako z\xc3\xa1kladn\xc3\xad v\xc4\x9bdeckou metodu[4][5] a p\xc5\x99\xc3\xa1l si \xe2\x80\x9em\xc3\xadt sv\xc4\x9bt, v n\xc4\x9bm\xc5\xbe ve\xc5\xa1ker\xc3\xa1 v\xc4\x9bdeck\xc3\xa1 literatura je online a v\xc5\xa1echny v\xc4\x9bdeck\xc3\xa9 \xc3\xbadaje jsou online a vz\xc3\xa1jemn\xc4\x9b spolupracuj\xc3\xad."[27]\n

Popularita pojmu "data science" v podnikatelsk\xc3\xa9m i akademick\xc3\xa9m prost\xc5\x99ed\xc3\xad prudce narostla, co\xc5\xbe ukazuje n\xc3\xa1r\xc5\xafst nab\xc3\xaddek pr\xc3\xa1ce pro datov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdce.[32] Nicm\xc3\xa9n\xc4\x9b mnoho kritick\xc3\xbdch akademick\xc3\xbdch pracovn\xc3\xadk\xc5\xaf a novin\xc3\xa1\xc5\x99\xc5\xaf nevid\xc3\xad \xc5\xbe\xc3\xa1dn\xc3\xbd rozd\xc3\xadl mezi datovou v\xc4\x9bdou a statistikou. Gil Press v \xc4\x8dasopise Forbes uvedl, \xc5\xbee data science je "buzzword" bez jasn\xc3\xa9 definice a jednodu\xc5\xa1e nahradil \xe2\x80\x9eobchodn\xc3\xad anal\xc3\xbdzu\xe2\x80\x9c v kontextech jako jsou postgradu\xc3\xa1ln\xc3\xad studijn\xc3\xad programy.[7] V r\xc3\xa1mci ot\xc3\xa1zek a odpov\xc4\x9bdi po sv\xc3\xa9 plen\xc3\xa1rn\xc3\xad p\xc5\x99edn\xc3\xa1\xc5\xa1ce na konferenci American Statistical Association aplikovan\xc3\xbd statistik Nate Silver \xc5\x99ekl: "Mysl\xc3\xadm, \xc5\xbee datov\xc3\xbd v\xc4\x9bdec je sexy term\xc3\xadn pro statistika. ... Statistika je odv\xc4\x9btv\xc3\xad v\xc4\x9bdy. Data scientist je lehce nadbyte\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xbd a lid\xc3\xa9 by nem\xc4\x9bli opou\xc5\xa1t\xc4\x9bt term\xc3\xadn statistik."[9] Stejn\xc4\x9b tak v podnikatelsk\xc3\xa9m sektoru \xc5\x99ada v\xc3\xbdzkumn\xc3\xadk\xc5\xaf a analytik\xc5\xaf uv\xc3\xa1d\xc3\xad, \xc5\xbee samotn\xc3\xad data scientist\xc3\xa9 zdaleka nesta\xc4\x8d\xc3\xad d\xc3\xa1t podnik\xc5\xafm skute\xc4\x8dnou konkuren\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xad v\xc3\xbdhodu[33] a pova\xc5\xbeuj\xc3\xad je za pouze jednu ze \xc4\x8dty\xc5\x99 velk\xc3\xbdch skupin profes\xc3\xad pot\xc5\x99ebn\xc3\xbdch k tomu, aby podniky efektivn\xc4\x9b vyu\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadvaly velk\xc3\xa1 data; jsou to analytici, data scientist\xc3\xa9, v\xc3\xbdvoj\xc3\xa1\xc5\x99i velk\xc3\xbdch dat a in\xc5\xbeen\xc3\xbd\xc5\x99i velk\xc3\xbdch dat.[34]\n

Na tuto kritiku p\xc5\x99i\xc5\xa1la \xc5\x99ada reakc\xc3\xad. V \xc4\x8dl\xc3\xa1nku ve Wall Street Journal v roce 2014 Irving Wladawsky-Berger porovn\xc3\xa1v\xc3\xa1 nad\xc5\xa1en\xc3\xad nad data science s \xc3\xbasvitem po\xc4\x8d\xc3\xadta\xc4\x8dov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdy. Argumentuje, \xc5\xbee data science stejn\xc4\x9b jako jak\xc3\xa1koli jin\xc3\xa1 interdisciplin\xc3\xa1rn\xc3\xad oblast vyu\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadv\xc3\xa1 metodiky a praktiky z cel\xc3\xa9 akademick\xc3\xa9 i komer\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xad sf\xc3\xa9ry, ale pak je p\xc5\x99etvo\xc5\x99\xc3\xad do nov\xc3\xa9 discipl\xc3\xadny. Uv\xc3\xa1d\xc3\xad ostr\xc3\xa9 kritiky, kte\xc5\x99\xc3\xad v minulosti \xc3\xbato\xc4\x8dili na informatiku, nyn\xc3\xad uzn\xc3\xa1vanou akademickou discipl\xc3\xadnou.[35] Podobn\xc4\x9b Vasant Dhar z New York University stejn\xc4\x9b jako \xc5\x99ada dal\xc5\xa1\xc3\xadch akademick\xc3\xbdch zast\xc3\xa1nc\xc5\xaf datov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdy[35] v prosinci 2013 konkr\xc3\xa9tn\xc4\x9bji argumentoval, \xc5\xbee se data science li\xc5\xa1\xc3\xad od st\xc3\xa1vaj\xc3\xadc\xc3\xad praxe anal\xc3\xbdzy dat ve v\xc5\xa1ech oborech, kter\xc3\xa1 se zam\xc4\x9b\xc5\x99uje pouze na vysv\xc4\x9btlen\xc3\xad datov\xc3\xbdch soubor\xc5\xaf. Data science hled\xc3\xa1 uplatniteln\xc3\xa9 a konzistentn\xc3\xad pravidelnosti v datech vhodn\xc3\xa9 prediktivn\xc3\xad vyu\xc5\xbeit\xc3\xad.[1] Tento praktick\xc3\xbd in\xc5\xbeen\xc3\xbdrsk\xc3\xbd c\xc3\xadl vyd\xc4\x9bluje datovou v\xc4\x9bdu mimo r\xc3\xa1mec tradi\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xad analytiky. Nyn\xc3\xad lze data hledat a vyu\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadvat i v t\xc4\x9bch discipl\xc3\xadn\xc3\xa1ch a aplikovan\xc3\xbdch oborech, kter\xc3\xa9 nemaj\xc3\xad spolehliv\xc3\xa9 teorie, jako jsou zdravotnictv\xc3\xad a spole\xc4\x8densk\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdy, a mohly by zde b\xc3\xbdt na z\xc3\xa1klad\xc4\x9b t\xc4\x9bchto dat vytvo\xc5\x99eny siln\xc3\xa9 prediktivn\xc3\xad modely.[1]\n

V podobn\xc3\xa9m duchu se v z\xc3\xa1\xc5\x99\xc3\xad 2015 vyj\xc3\xa1d\xc5\x99il profesor ze Stanfordu David Donoho. P\xc5\x99itom odm\xc3\xadtl t\xc5\x99i zjednodu\xc5\xa1uj\xc3\xadc\xc3\xad a zav\xc3\xa1d\xc4\x9bj\xc3\xadc\xc3\xad definice data science, je\xc5\xbe b\xc3\xbdvaj\xc3\xad p\xc5\x99edm\xc4\x9btem kritiky.[36] Za prv\xc3\xa9 se podle Donoha datov\xc3\xa1 v\xc4\x9bda ned\xc3\xa1 ztoto\xc5\xbenit s velk\xc3\xbdmi daty, nebo\xc5\xa5 velikost datov\xc3\xa9ho souboru nen\xc3\xad krit\xc3\xa9riem pro rozli\xc5\xa1ov\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad mezi datovou v\xc4\x9bdou a statistikou.[36] Za druh\xc3\xa9 datov\xc3\xa1 v\xc4\x9bda nen\xc3\xad definov\xc3\xa1na v\xc3\xbdpo\xc4\x8detn\xc3\xadmi schopnostmi zpracov\xc3\xa1n\xc3\xad velk\xc3\xbdch soubor\xc5\xaf dat, nebo\xc5\xa5 tyto mo\xc5\xbenosti jsou ji\xc5\xbe obecn\xc4\x9b pou\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadv\xc3\xa1ny pro anal\xc3\xbdzy ve v\xc5\xa1ech oborech.[36] Za t\xc5\x99et\xc3\xad datov\xc3\xa1 v\xc4\x9bda je siln\xc4\x9b aplikovan\xc3\xbd obor, v n\xc4\x9bm\xc5\xbe akademick\xc3\xa9 programy v sou\xc4\x8dasn\xc3\xa9 dob\xc4\x9b dostate\xc4\x8dn\xc4\x9b nep\xc5\x99ipravuj\xc3\xad dorost, proto\xc5\xbee \xc5\x99ada absolventsk\xc3\xbdch program\xc5\xaf zav\xc3\xa1d\xc4\x9bj\xc3\xadc\xc3\xadm zp\xc5\xafsobem inzeruje svou analytickou a statistickou v\xc3\xbduku jako podstatu kurikula pro datovou v\xc4\x9bdu.[36][37] Donoho jako\xc5\xbeto statistik spolu s mnoha kolegy jeho oboru podporuje roz\xc5\xa1\xc3\xad\xc5\x99en\xc3\xad rozsahu u\xc4\x8diva adept\xc5\xaf datov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdy.[36] Tak\xc3\xa9 John Chambers \xc5\xbe\xc3\xa1d\xc3\xa1 statistiky, aby p\xc5\x99ijali inkluzivn\xc3\xad koncepci u\xc4\x8den\xc3\xad z dat,[38] a William Cleveland po\xc5\xbeaduje, aby se up\xc5\x99ednost\xc5\x88ovalo vytv\xc3\xa1\xc5\x99en\xc3\xad predikc\xc3\xad nad vysv\xc4\x9btluj\xc3\xadc\xc3\xadmi teoriemi.[21] Spole\xc4\x8dnou viz\xc3\xad t\xc4\x9bchto statistik\xc5\xaf je st\xc3\xa1le obs\xc3\xa1hlej\xc5\xa1\xc3\xad aplikovan\xc3\xbd obor, kter\xc3\xa9 p\xc5\x99er\xc5\xafst\xc3\xa1 hranice tradi\xc4\x8dn\xc3\xad statistiky.\n

Budoucnost datov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdy vid\xc3\xad Donoho v neust\xc3\xa1le rostouc\xc3\xadm prost\xc5\x99ed\xc3\xad otev\xc5\x99en\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdy, kde jsou datov\xc3\xa9 soubory vyu\xc5\xbe\xc3\xadvan\xc3\xa9 akademick\xc3\xbdmi publikacemi p\xc5\x99\xc3\xadstupn\xc3\xa9 v\xc5\xa1em v\xc3\xbdzkumn\xc3\xadk\xc5\xafm.[36] Americk\xc3\xbd n\xc3\xa1rodn\xc3\xad zdravotn\xc3\xad \xc3\xbastav ji\xc5\xbe ozn\xc3\xa1mil pl\xc3\xa1ny na zv\xc3\xbd\xc5\xa1en\xc3\xad reprodukovatelnosti a pr\xc5\xafhlednosti v\xc3\xbdzkumn\xc3\xbdch \xc3\xbadaj\xc5\xaf.[39] N\xc4\x9bkter\xc3\xa9 velk\xc3\xa9 odborn\xc3\xa9 \xc4\x8dasopisy ji\xc5\xbe n\xc3\xa1sleduj\xc3\xad tento trend.[40][41] T\xc3\xadmto zp\xc5\xafsobem budoucnost datov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdy nejen p\xc5\x99ekra\xc4\x8duje hranice statistick\xc3\xa9 teorie co do rozsahu a metodologie, ale data science znamen\xc3\xa1 v\xc3\xbdzvu sou\xc4\x8dasn\xc3\xbdm akademick\xc3\xbdm a v\xc3\xbdzkumn\xc3\xbdm paradigmat\xc5\xafm.[36] Jak dod\xc3\xa1v\xc3\xa1 Donoho, "rozsah a dopad datov\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bdy se v nadch\xc3\xa1zej\xc3\xadc\xc3\xadch desetilet\xc3\xadch bude nad\xc3\xa1le roz\xc5\xa1i\xc5\x99ovat, nebo\xc5\xa5 v\xc4\x9bdeck\xc3\xa1 data i data o samotn\xc3\xa9 v\xc4\x9bd\xc4\x9b se stanou v\xc5\xa1eobecn\xc4\x9b dostupn\xc3\xbdmi".[36]\n
