Computational fields of study are areas of research in an existing field using the power of computation, and which are usually named for that. Computational fields of study as a group are sometimes also be referred to as Computational X[1].

  1. ^ How to Teach Computational Thinking by Stephen Wolfram, Stephen Wolfram Blog, September 7, 2016.
